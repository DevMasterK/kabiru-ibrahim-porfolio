Welcome to Kabiru Ibrahim's Portfolio

With over 20 years of active involvement in the world of tennis, Kabiru Ibrahim is a seasoned International Tennis Coach, Professional Player, and Certified Tennis Official who has excelled at the highest levels of the sport.


As a globally certified GPTA coach and Tennis Official, Kabiru brings a wealth of expertise in coaching elite players and officiating at prestigious international tournaments, including the Qatar ExxonMobil Open and the Qatar Total Open. His hands-on experience working with athletes of all levels—from emerging talents to seasoned professionals—has positioned him as a sought-after mentor for those looking to refine their technique, strengthen their mental game, and compete at the highest levels.


In addition to his extensive experience in the tennis arena, Kabiru has recently expanded his qualifications by earning CPR, AED, and First Aid certification through NHCPS Save a Life, scoring an impressive 100% on his exam. He has also achieved certification in Basic Life Support (BLS) through CME University. These essential life-saving skills further enhance his ability to ensure the safety and well-being of athletes, both on and off the court, making him a more versatile and valuable coach in any setting.


Kabiru's approach to coaching combines personalized, results-driven programs with a deep passion for the game, focusing on technical skills, physical conditioning, and mental resilience. He has trained and guided players in regions as diverse as Nigeria and Qatar, making him a truly global figure in the tennis world. His ability to connect with athletes, motivate them, and elevate their game has earned him recognition across continents.


Whether you're an aspiring junior player, an advanced competitor, or an academy looking for top-tier coaching talent, Kabiru Ibrahim’s proven track record of success, professional accolades, and passion for tennis development make him the ideal coach to help you reach your goals. His vision is simple: to foster the next generation of champions and elevate tennis on a global stage.
